Home > Parks > Clarkston > Independence Oaks

Independence Oaks is on Northern Sashabaw road, in Northern Oakland County. It offers many miles of walking trails, which are suitable for light to moderate mountain biking. Some of the hills are quite steep. It's beautiful year round and offeres many anemities, like bathrooms and drinking fountains located around the park.
