Clinton River Trail also deserves a special place. To date, I've set several personal bests on it. Since it extends onto Orchard-Macomb Trail, it's one of the longest trails in Michigan. Prior to riding on the Gaylord Mackinaw trail, this was the longest trail I've ever done. The last time I did it, I also set a personal speed record. 35 miles in about 1.5 hours(One way, I wasn't as fast on the way back).
Clinton River trail itself starts on Opdyke, just south of the silver dome. It continues through Auburn Hills, Rochester, and many other small cities before heading into Macomb county. It too was a rail to trail.
