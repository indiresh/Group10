Home > Parks > Lake Orion >Polly Ann

Polly Ann Trail is an impressive feat. It is a multi-city multi-county effort rail to trail. Total Length is some more than 50 feet. Unfortunately, I know of no one who has ever done it. Why? Well, north of Oakland County, starting exactly at the Lapeer border, the trail is not maintained. That means it's barely recognizeable as a trail. In fact, some areas I would call more difficult than Pontiac Lake. But, the Oakland County path is well maintained, with ~2/3 of it being paved. There is a water stop along the way at Oxford Frosty Boy. I enjoy this trail, as it's rather scenic. I've tried to include some high points in the picture reel.

As with the other extremely long paths, this one is split up.
