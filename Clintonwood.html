Home > Parks > Clarkston > Clintonwood

Clintonwood is a park just South West of Independence Oaks. It offers very little in terms of bike trails, but was included because it there are a few, it's also in close proximity to many other trails and quite a bit of nature. Clintonwood also has a spray park.

The spray park is now a huge feature in this park, while they have let other life long memories, like the skate rink, go to waste. There are now weeds as big as me growing there, making it impossible to use.
